Bible Reading

Theme: Arise and Shine
Philippians 3:12-14
Philippians 4:13

Sunday's Sermon
  • WE CAN DO ALL THINGS THROUGH CHRIST WHO STRENGTHENS US.
  • Sometimes we make great plans for our lives, but God's plan is the best plan.
  • Paul, in saying that "he can do all things....," did not mean that his life was an easy one.
  • In Chapter 3, Paul says "he is pressing...," meaning that even if his life could have been tough, he was still pressing on.
  • Even in waiting, Paul was pressing on.
  • "To press" means to exercise strength.
  • In front of Paul, there was prison, there was persecution and all manner of resistance, but he kept pressing on.
  • Beyond the stumbling blocks or difficulties or destiny lie our blessings.
  • When God is taking you somewhere, He is not going to give you anything little.
  • We should press on, because it is not by our own might, but it is by God's strength.
  • We have to remind our problems that we are children of God.
  • There is nothing impossible to one who believes.
  • Trials will always come our way- God does not give us immunity against trials, but He gives us strength to overcome them.
  • We have to be tried and by that we are strengthened and proven strong.
  • God has given us so much strength that He knows we can overcome what may come our way.
  • God will not permit trials beyond our capacity to come our way.
  • Paul talks about being content- meaning he was ok whatever was happening to him.
